About Me
I am a Research Project Officer of Rapid-Rich Object Search (ROSE) Lab, under NTU-PKU Joint Research Institute in Nanyang Technology University (NTU), Singapore. I have been led several industrial projects about computer vision, as the manager & developer. Meantime, I am also a PhD candidate (since 2020) in School of EEE, NTU, supervised by Prof. Alex Kot. I also closely work with Dr. Li Haoliang. My thesis topic will be generalized face anti-spoofing and presentation attack detection. Before I joined NTU, I was a B. Eng student in Shenzhen University, China, advised my Dr. Chen Changsheng.

Research interest
Currently, I thesis topic is face presentation/spoofing detection, a.k.a face anti-spoofing. I am interested in using domain generalization techniques to improve face anti-spoofing.
Community activity
I server as reviewers for several top journals or conferences in the field of machine learning and computer vision: IEEE T-IP, IEEE T-IFS, IEEE T-CSVT, IEEE Access, IJCV, ICME, AAAI, etc.
I was as an invited speaker in 13th IEEE International Workshop on Information Forensics and Security (2021).
